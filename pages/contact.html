Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a 20-mile radius of our bakery for a fee based on distance. Delivery is strongly recommended for tiered cakes and delicate designs.

Can I place an order over the phone?

Absolutely! You can place orders by calling us at (555) 123-4567 during our business hours. For custom cake orders, we recommend placing your order at least one week in advance.

Do you have gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan baked goods daily. Our menu changes regularly, so please call ahead if you're looking for specific items or have severe allergies.

Do you cater for events or corporate functions?

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. We can create custom dessert tables, breakfast pastry platters, and more. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to discuss your catering needs.
